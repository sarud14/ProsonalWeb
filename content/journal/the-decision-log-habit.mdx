## Decisions decay

Six months later, nobody remembers why a choice was made — only that changing it feels risky. A short log captures the context while it is still cheap to write.

## Cheap to write, costly to skip

Two paragraphs at decision time — context and trade-off — saved us countless re-litigations. It is the highest-leverage writing I do.
