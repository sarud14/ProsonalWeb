## Unlearn the waterfall

The hardest part was not syntax — it was unlearning the client-fetch-then-render habit. We spent more time on where code runs than on how to write it.

## Make the boundary visible

Once the team could see the server/client line in every file, the patterns clicked. Naming and folder conventions did most of the teaching.
